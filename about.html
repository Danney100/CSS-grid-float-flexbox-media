ABOUT

As a contracted Backend Engineer at Trym, regulatory compliance and management software for cannabis growers (https://trym.io), you will help us continue to move our platform forward through a period of rapid scale. In this role, you’ll have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on Trym’s product development efforts, and work alongside an incredible team of skilled engineers. Role responsibilities - Help design overall architecture of our web application - Maintain quality and ensure responsiveness of applications - Collaborate with the rest of the engineering and product team to design and launch new features - Maintain code integrity and organization - Understanding and implementation of security and data protection

Requirements - 3+ years of professional experience as a developer - Experienced in Python with expert knowledge working with Django framework - Proficient in developing asynchronous task queues with Celery - Experience working with Postgresql - Senior level knowledge of API design and development - Detailed understanding of continuous integration (CI) practice and testing disciplines - High-level understanding of Heroku and AWS platforms - Proficient in Git and Git Flow repository operations - A positive relationship with cannabis products and related industries
